The pool was a true highlight—perfectly positioned to soak in the stunning sunsets, surrounded by the sounds of nature.

Relaxing in the hot tub after a day of exploring was an absolute dream, and the outdoor shower added a refreshing, spa-like experience. The home itself was beautifully designed with an open-concept layout, featuring a massive chef’s kitchen that made preparing meals a joy. Fully stocked with top-of-the-line appliances and plenty of space, it was ideal for cooking up fresh local ingredients. The spacious living areas provided a perfect space to unwind, while the private bedrooms offered comfort and tranquility, each with its own unique view. Great location close to good beaches and city center.
